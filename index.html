id="TheOrigin"> The Origin

The term meme, is a relatively new word, having been coined in the late 90's and is older than the world wide web itself. The term was originally coined from the greek word mimeme, meme for short. Dawkins was an evolutionary biologist and the term was originally meant for the concept that ideas evolve and change similar to genes and organisms themselves, and hence the term meme was born.
